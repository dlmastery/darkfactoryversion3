# RASCI

Use these defaults and tailor per project.

| Area | Responsible | Accountable | Supporting | Consulted | Informed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Product intent | product expert | human owner | requirements lead | users, domain expert | delivery team |
| Architecture | system architect | human technical owner | DDD, MDA, SRE | security, data | product |
| Code change | implementation lead | technical owner | test, security | architect | product |
| Artifact acceptance | artifact author | governance owner | expert reviewers | human owner | project team |
| Production release | release manager | human production owner | SRE, security | product, support | stakeholders |
| Incident response | SRE lead | human production owner | security, maintainer | product | stakeholders |

Human accountability is mandatory for production, compliance waivers, high-risk security decisions, and threshold reductions unless the organization explicitly delegates it.
