# Standards Baseline

- ISO/IEC/IEEE 12207:2017: software life cycle process baseline.
- ISO/IEC/IEEE 15289:2019: life-cycle information item content baseline.
- ISO/IEC 42001:2023: AI management and governance baseline.
- NIST SP 800-218 SSDF 1.1 final: secure SDLC baseline.
- OWASP SAMM: software assurance maturity baseline.
- OMG MDA and UML 2.5.1: model-driven and modeling baseline when selected.
- CMMI V3.0: maturity, measurement, and improvement baseline when selected.

Record the date the baseline was checked and rerun standards watch for regulated or long-lived programs.
