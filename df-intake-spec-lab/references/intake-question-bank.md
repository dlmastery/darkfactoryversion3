# Intake Question Bank

Ask only questions that materially reduce risk. Prefer assumptions for low-risk gaps. For governed work, ask these inside the formal interrogation protocol and capture each material answer with an answer ID.

Product:
- What problem is this solving?
- Who owns the outcome?
- Who are the primary users?
- What is explicitly out of scope?

Quality:
- What does success look like?
- Which non-functional requirements matter most: latency, scale, availability, security, privacy, accessibility, cost, maintainability?
- What company stack or standards must be followed?

Delivery:
- Is this greenfield or brownfield?
- What must be handed to humans for maintenance?
- What is the risk class?

Escalate if production, legal, compliance, safety, money movement, privacy, or irreversible migration risk is present.
