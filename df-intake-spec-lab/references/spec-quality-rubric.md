# Spec Quality Rubric

Score each item 0 to 4.

1. Goal is concrete.
2. Stakeholders and owners are identified.
3. Users and user journeys are clear.
4. Scope and out-of-scope are explicit.
5. Functional requirements are atomic and testable.
6. Non-functional requirements are explicit.
7. Constraints and assumptions are separated.
8. Acceptance criteria are measurable.
9. Security, privacy, and compliance needs are visible.
10. Production and maintenance expectations are visible.
11. Domain language is consistent.
12. Risks and unknowns are listed.
13. Success metrics are measurable.
14. Handoff needs are described.
15. The next lifecycle node can proceed without guessing.

Default pass: 90 percent for low risk, 96 percent for high risk.
