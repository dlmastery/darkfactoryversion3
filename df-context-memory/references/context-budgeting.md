# Context Budgeting

Use small context packs:

- 10 percent objective and current stage.
- 20 percent requirements and decisions.
- 20 percent relevant code or artifacts.
- 20 percent risks, open questions, and constraints.
- 20 percent verification and evidence state.
- 10 percent next action and owner.

Refresh the pack after long pauses, human edits, failed gates, phase changes, or major design decisions.
