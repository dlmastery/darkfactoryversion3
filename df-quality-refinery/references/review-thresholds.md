# Review Thresholds

Default:
- Governance: 98 percent.
- Each selected expert: 96 percent.
- Traceability: 100 percent for mandatory links.
- Security: zero unresolved critical or high findings.

Verdicts:
- Pass: every threshold met, residual risk acceptable.
- Conditional pass: low/medium risk accepted by owner with expiry.
- Revise: direction is valid, but required fixes remain.
- Fail: unsafe, unsupported, unverifiable, or below threshold.
- Escalate: human decision required.
