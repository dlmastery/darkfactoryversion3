# Evidence Schema

Evidence records should include:

- id.
- type: test, build, review, static-analysis, threat-model, performance, release, handoff, approval.
- subject.
- produced_by.
- produced_at.
- command_or_method.
- result.
- linked_requirements.
- linked_artifacts.
- linked_risks.
- residual_risk.

Evidence is not proof unless it is inspectable by a fresh reviewer.
